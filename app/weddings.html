Weddings & Special Events

  Are you seeking a one-of-a-kind musical experience for your wedding day? Beck and Jonathan can help you create the themed atmosphere you wish for, with emotive, sensitive music before/during the ceremony.They can gently slant the theme towards classical, folk, hauntingly Celtic, darkly romantic gothic, or medieval/renaissance. Your wish is their command, and they shall do their best to exceed your desires and provide you with wedding music to remember as a very special part of your most precious day.

Beck and Jonathan have a repertoire of romantic, beautiful songs, and they are also very happy to learn and do their own romantic interpretation of your favourite, special song/s, especially for you.The fee is determined by how many songs they will be providing throughout your wedding ceremony, and also the travel expenses from their base in West Sussex to your wedding venue. Please do get in touch to discuss your requirements and to ask for a quote.
     
  For further information or to discuss your needs, please email us at info@acousticgoth.com. We look forward to hearing from you!
