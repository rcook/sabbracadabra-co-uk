Demos

A selection of demo samples from some of the Black Sabbath classics played by Sabbra Cadabra are available here for your listening pleasure. You will need an MP3 player in order to listen to these demos. If you can't already do this, then a player can be downloaded from http://www.winamp.com/.
  • Behind the Wall of Sleep (Intro and First Verse) (0min58sec, 457kB MP3)
    A classic from Sabbath's 1970 debut album and a great demonstration of Matt's vocal ability.
  • Snowblind (Second Verse and Solo) (1min51sec, 870kB MP3)
    From 1972's Vol. 4 album.
  • Children Of The Grave (Last Verse and Solo) (1min25sec, 667kB MP3)
    A much-loved classic from the album Masters Of Reality.
  • Paranoid (Intro, First and Second Verse and Bridge) (1min00sec, 472kB MP3)
    Possibly one of the best known and most frequently covered rock songs ever.
