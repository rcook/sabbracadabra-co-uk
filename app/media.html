Beginner Lessons

     

Video 1 - Parts of the Guitar

Beginners' guide to the names of parts of the electric guitar. It is intended to give a basic insight into the correct terms used for different components of the electric guitar.
 

Video 2 - Guitar Terms

Beginners' guide to the terms used working around the guitar. which way is "up the strings"? Which way is "down the strings"? Or the neck? This video is aimed at complete beginners and novices or those interested in the correct use of terms but perhaps haven't worried about them until now!
     
 

Video 3 - Guitar Playing Position

This video is aimed at beginners and is intended to give a basic insight into good sitting positions for playing and practising guitar. The points made are relevant to both electric and acoustic guitar.
     
 

Video 4 - Use of Arms & Hands Part 1

This video looks at the best use of the arms and hands on the guitar. Not only to ensure the best playing position but also to avoid causing physical damage to the arms. Part 1 focuses on warm ups and use of the picking /strumming hand.
     
 

Video 5 - Use of Arms & Hands Part 2

This video looks at the best use of the arms and hands on the guitar. Not only to ensure the best playing position but also to avoid causing physical damage to the arms. Part 2 focuses on use of the fretting hand.
     
 

Video 6 - Beginners' Strumming Part 1

This video is aimed at beginners and looks at learning to strum the guitar accurately and correctly. It covers both plectrum/pick and finger strumming as well as use of the metronome and working to a click.
     
 

Video 7 - Beginners' Chords - A, D and E

This video covers the basics of some first chords for beginners, looking at the A major, D major and E major chords. It considers the best way to change between the chords and also looks at different fingerings where relevant. This video is intended for beginners and assumes you have knowledge of chord diagrams, basic guitar notation and guitar technique
