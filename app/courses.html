Courses

All Circus Bizarre courses and tuition are individually based, taking into account individual tastes and needs and ensuring above all else the tuition is enjoyable as well as informative. (Please note that all lessons and courses described here have an individual cost.)

  • One to One

     
Each lesson is tailored to individual needs, concentrating on the client's interests and tastes to determine the course of the tuition. Programmes are discussed for each client to ensure they learn what they want in the most expedient manner. These sessions can be easy going and fun, learning at a leisurely pace or for those who prefer a more "hardcore" approach, the sessions can be more intense and demanding.

 

  • Group tuition/Masterclasses

Masterclasses and beginner group sessions are available with demand. The masterclasses can cover a wide range of areas of interest from different technical approaches to close examination of various musical genres, bands or musicians. Keep an eye on this site for more details!

 

  • Internet Tuition (via Skype)

We are really pleased to now be able to offer our specialised lessons via Skype; meaning we can help you improve no matter where you are in the world! Please note, these lessons are in English only). To find out more about these or to arrange a session, contact us at info@circusbizarre.co.uk.

 

  • Jam Sessions*

One of the most popular services at Circus Bizarre Guitar Tuition are the jam sessions*. These can either be one to one or in one of the monthly group sessions of 4 to 5 players. Not only good fun but also a great opportunity to experiment and trade licks with other guitarists. 

* Please note, the jam sessions are only available to current students with Circus BizarreGuitar Tuition.

 

  • Online Video Lessons

If you're just lookinng for some quick advice or are would like to do a little self study before taking on lessons, why not try some of the FREE guitar video lessons available on this website. Simply click on the Videos tab on the main menu bar.

 

Please feel free to contact us about any of these by using the "Get in Touch" button above.
