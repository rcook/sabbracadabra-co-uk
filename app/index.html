Welcome

"A professional guitar tuition service that I'd recommend to anyone"
               - Paul Bielatowicz, Guitar Techniques magazine & Carl Palmer Legacy Band

Welcome to Circus Bizarre Guitar Tuition - professional guitar lessons and music theory lessons in the Worthing - Lancing - Shoreham - Brighton area.

Working from a dedicated teaching studio in Lancing, Circus Bizarre is dedicated to first class guitar lessons for all levels of player ages 12 to adult.

Lessons are provided in either an easy, relaxed and fun environment or a hard-working, demanding one depending on the needs and desires of the client. So whether you simply wish to start to learn to play guitar or are a more experienced player looking for coaching, mentoring or to hone your abilities, Circus Bizarre can help.

We specialise in hard rock and heavy metal playing techniques as well as offering the following services:

  • One on one guitar lessons  (group tuition available with demand);
  • Learning programmes tailored to individual needs;
  • Tuition from QUALIFIED teacher and musician.
  • Masterclasses and workshops covering various styles and techniques;
  • Studies in plectrum and fingerstyle playing;
  • Music theory lessons (inc. grade 5 theory);
  • Advice on band management, performance, equipment maintenance and set up;
  • Progress and demo CD recording;
  • Advice & teaching for tutors available.

To aid players of all levels, we are also building a catalogue of video guitar lessons on the new Videos page which we'e looking to develop to assist with all matters from the bare basics through to advanced scales, chords and concepts.

Please browse the website and if you have any questions or require further information on courses or other guitar lessons please contact us through the contacts page on this site. We look forward to hearing from you!

 

Jon Kershaw (Head of tuition)
