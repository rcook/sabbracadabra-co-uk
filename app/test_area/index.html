GUITAR LESSONS: BEGINNER TO ADVANCED * AGES 12 TO ADULT

Since establishing Circus Bizarre Guitar Tuition in 2003, tutor Jon Kershaw has earned a reputation for high quality, professional guitar tuition and coaching. Providing professional guitar lessons and music theory lessons in the Worthing, Lancing, Shoreham and Brighton area. Working from a dedicated teaching studio in Lancing, West Sussex, Circus Bizarre is dedicated to first class guitar lessons for all levels of player ages 12 to adult. Lessons can be provided in either an easy, relaxed and fun environment or a hard-working, demanding one depending on the needs and desires of the client. So whether you simply wish to start to learn to play guitar or are a more experienced player looking for coaching, mentoring or to hone your abilities, Circus Bizarre can help.
