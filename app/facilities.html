Facilities

The Circus Bizarre studio is currently based in Lancing, near Worthing.

Recording facilities allow clients to record themselves against pre-produced or self produced backing tracks with either CD or audio cassette output.

The studio is capable of replaying almost all possible formats for music:

  • CD
  • Audio cassette
  • Mini disc
  • Data disc
  • DVD
  • MP3

So clients have no excuses for not bringing along their favourite music to listen to and learn from!

All equipment for sessions is provided including guitar and amplification.

Circus Bizarre is also able to allow students to hire guitars and amplification if they wish to start learning without the heavy investment of new equipment first
