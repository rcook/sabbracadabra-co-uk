Out On The Town

06-12-14 Jonathan Busking in Littlehampton

Jonathan was out busking on the streets of Littlehampton today helping to promote both the "Small Business Saturday" initative as well as the release of his new Remnants album. "It was freezing today. You can see in the photo that I was wearing fingerless gloves (kindly donated by one of the stall holders) to keep my hands moving. But it was fun chatting with everyone and having a laugh with various stallholders. I don't think I made much from the actual busking though!

New Album "Remnants" Out Now

*******************************************************************************

23-11-14 New Release from Jonathan Kershaw

The new album "Remnants" by Jonathan Kershaw was released today as a special limited edition CD. The album is not a follow up to " Bizarre Tales" but a collection of the traditional Celtic pieces Jonathan uses in his live sets. This limited edition CD digipak version contains a bonus track featuring the incredible voice of Beck Sian together with Jonathan's original material. Beck and Jonathan have been touring together for the "Beautiful Ghosts & Bizarre Tales" tour and this is their first actual recorded material together. The download and standard versions of the album will not contain this track.

You can have a listen to some full and sample tracks in the on-site Listening Room (opens in a new window).

Beautiful Ghosts & Bizarre Tales Tour 2013-2014

28-05-13 Joint tour of the UK with Beck Siàn

Starting in July this year, Jonathan will be on tour around the UK (and hopefully beyond) with renowned singer/songwriter Beck Siàn. Beck’s music has been described as “Ethereal Ghost-Folk”, with a tendency towards the darker, supernatural ballads and nostalgic songs of love and loss, whether performing her original compositions or stirring interpretations of traditional and Celtic songs. Her voice is rather astonishing. Influenced by a famous relative (Kate Bush), Beck has found her own voice, which can be heartbreakingly wistful one moment and a window-shattering soprano the next.

See the Live page for details of dates.

Album Release

29-08-12: New Album Release Date - 31st August 2012

After numerous setbacks and delays, the new album entitled "Bizarre Tales" is scheduled for imminent release on Friday 31st August on the Circus Bizarre Music label. The album features 11 original tracks with guest peformances from vocalists Laura Fauvel and Karina McGrath. The album was recorded at Circus Bizarre Studio in West Sussex and mixed and mastered by Chris Jones at Boxer studio in Belfast. The album will available to purchase as a download from 31st August and available in a CD digipack very shortly after.

Album Artwork

21-05-12: Original Artwork by Rachel Cavalier and Photos by Tracey Swain

The packaging for the album is proving as exciting as the music on it. The cover artwork is an original and bespoke piece by artist Rachel Cavalier. Not only has she created this piece specifically for the album but also provided several hand sketches for use within the packaging.

The packaging has been further enhanced by the kind use of photographs by renowned landscape photographer Tracey Swain. These can be found within the gatefold and booklet. You can checkout and order prints of Tracey's work from her own website.
