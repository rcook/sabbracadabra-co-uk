Welcome

Welcome to Circus Bizarre Guitar Tuition - professional guitar and music theory lessons in the Worthing - Lancing - Shoreham - Brighton area.

Circus Bizarre is dedicated to first class guitar tuition for all levels of player ages 14 to adult.

We specialise in hard rock and heavy metal playing techniques as well as offering the following services:

  • One on one tuition (group tuition available with demand);
  • Learning programmes tailored to individual needs;
  • Tuition from QUALIFIED teacher and musician.
  • Masterclasses and workshops covering various styles and technqiues;
  • Studies in plectrum and fingerstyle playing;
  • Music theory tuition (inc. grade 5 theory);
  • Advice on band management, performance, equipment maintenance and set up;
  • Progress and demo CD recording;
  • Equipment hire for new learners.
  • Advice & teaching for tutors available.

Please browse the website and if you have any questions or require further information on courses or tuition please contact us through the contacts page on this site. We look forward to hearing from you!
