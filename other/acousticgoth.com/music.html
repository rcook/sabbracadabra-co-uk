Haunting, dark sounds with Gothic and Celtic origins...

Beck and Jonathan came together to discover mutual origins in their separate work. Both influenced through time by both dark, rock music and seductive traditional sounds, their individual music not only compliments one another but works to create a serene and preternatural atmosphere with enough catchy hooks to keep audiences engaged.
