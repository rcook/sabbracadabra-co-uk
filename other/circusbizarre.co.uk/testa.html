Clients & Testamonials

Some thoughts and examples from those who have experienced Circus Bizarre Guitar Tuition either as a student or through professional association:

 

Paul Bielatowicz - Guitar Techniques & Carl Palmer's ELP Legacy Band

"A professional guitar tuition service that I'd recommend to anyone"


Paul is a virtuoso player from the UK. He is known for his work touring with Carl Palmer (ex-ELP) and Neal Morse (ex- Spock's Beard) and has also taught at the Academy of Contemporary Music in Guildford, UK and the Brighton Institute Of Modern Music in Brighton, UK. He recently released his solo album Preludes & Etudes showcasing his skills with his own guitar arrangements for some popular and challenging classical pieces. You can find out more about Paul and purchase the album direct from his own website PaulBielatowicz.com.

 

Simon - Bleed Again

"....before I met Jon I was self taught for a couple of year. I soon reached a bottleneck in my playing in all aspects - inspiration, technique and theory. He quickly 'took me under his wing' and got my playing rolling full steam ahead! Many years later I am still learning and feel my playing has progressed massively since my first lesson! I'm am forever grateful to Jon for helping my progress..."


Simon is guitarist and main songwriter for Worthing based band, "Bleed Again". Their recent EP "Surrender. No." has been causing quite a stir on the south coast with it's combination of ultra tight, face smashing rhythms and catchy melodies. Check them out at http://www.bleedagainband.co.uk/
