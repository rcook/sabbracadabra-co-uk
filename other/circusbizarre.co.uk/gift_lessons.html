Guitar Lesson Gifts For Christmas

With lessons in studio or online via Skype, anyone can benefit from professional guitar lessons

A new guitar is one of the most common gifts given and received during the festive season. Whether is is a complete beginner looking to make a start at tackling the instrument or a more experienced player looking to upgrade their current axe or add a new gem to their collection, a guitar is a true gift for life.

So why not give the recipient the opportunity to make full use of what can be quite an expensive investment and purchase them some professional guitar lessons as a further gift?

For Beginners

This is an excellent way for a complete beginner to get a good start on the instrument with professional tuition by a qualified tutor rather than relying on "friends' advice" and Youtube videos (which can often be misleading). Learning the right techniques from the outset can make a huge difference to how long it takes to master the instrument and can avoid picking up bad techniques which may be restrictive and possibly even damaging.

For Intermediate Players

Guitar lessons can provide an opportunity to brush up on some techniques that may have proved problematic to date or to look at new more advanced concepts. There's no end to learning in guitar and music so it's always great to get new ideas and advice to work with.

For Advanced Players

Even the most advanced player can find benefit in working with their peers or through dedicated coaching pushing them to greater things. Guitar sessions provide them the opportunity to venture further, hone skills through scheduled training and discuss their technique and musicality in great detail.
