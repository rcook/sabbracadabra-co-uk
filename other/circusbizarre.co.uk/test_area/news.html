New "Power-Up" Guitar Sessions Online

If you're simply in need of some quick advice or assistance with any aspect of your playing and would like to discuss the matter, why not arrange a "power-up" session of 10 or 25mins? These can cover aspects from music theory to guitar technique and provide you with a direct video session with tutor, Jon Kershaw, to look at the issue with you. These sessions are ideal for players who are learning remotely, are self-tutored, are looking for advice on specific issues or perhaps simply can't afford the outlay for continuous professional guitar lessons. They are also ideal for more experienced players looking to find ways or advice on honing their abilities to take them on to new levels.
