18 March 2014 - Webcam "Power-Up" Sessions Online

   
If you're simply in need of some quick advice or assistance with any aspect of your playing and would like to discuss the matter, why not arrange a "power-up" session of 10 or 25mins? These can cover aspects from music theory to guitar technique and provide you with a direct video session with tutor, Jon Kershaw, to look at the issue with you.  
These sessions are ideal for players who are learning remotely, are self-tutored, are looking for advice on specific issues or perhaps simply can't afford the outlay for continuous professional guitar lessons. They are also ideal for more experienced players looking to find ways or advice on honing their abilities to take them on to new levels.

 

18 March 2014 - Circus Bizarre Guitar Tuition Membership

   
It is now possible to become a "Circus Bizarre Guitar Tuition Member". The benefits of becoming a member include reductions in the cost of in-studio lessons, removal of the 24 hour cancellation fee, discounts on in-house tutorials, masterclasses and workshops and access to the new (soon to be added) "Members' Area" on the website.
The new members' area will include videos, backing tracks, technique exercises and guitar teaching material not available to non-members.

10 Oct 2014 - CBGT Student's Band on Terrorizer Magazine Cover CD


  Circus Bizarre student Simon Williams' band "Bleed Again" have been featured on the cover CD of this month's "Terrorizer" magazine. Simon has been developing the band throughout his time studying at CBGT and the last year has seen some real advances in the south coast based band. With a crushingly heavy yet melodic sound, the band have already released an EP and album but it was this year's release of a new E.P. "Surrender. No." which has seen a sudden growth in the band's musical maturity and fan following. It is the title track "Surrender. No." that features on the Terrorizer magazine CD. You can get more information on the band at their website www.bleedagainband.co.uk

Terrorizer magazine is a UK heavy metal magazine specialising in extreme forms of the genre. It has a readership (at time of print) of around 11-12000 and is recognised as one of the current leading magazines for extreme metal music coverage and support for underground and unsigned bands.
www.terrorizer.com
